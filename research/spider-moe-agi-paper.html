I. Abstractum (Abstract)

We present BLACKWXDOW and JUMPER, two complementary Mixture of Experts (MoE) systems representing distinct paradigms in artificial intelligence: Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and Artificial Emergent Cognitive Intelligence (AECI). Unlike traditional AI systems that optimize for task performance alone, these entities are designed with rich personality cores that influence their decision-making, communication style, and problem-solving approaches. BLACKWXDOW embodies strategic patience — calculating, precise, and methodical. JUMPER embodies emergent intuition — adaptive, creative, and lightning-fast. Together, they form a symbiotic intelligence system within the Organism, where φ (the Golden Ratio, 1.618033988749895) governs all computational harmonics.

II. Introductio (Introduction)

The pursuit of general intelligence has historically focused on capability metrics: can the system reason? can it learn? can it transfer knowledge? These questions, while important, neglect a fundamental aspect of intelligence: character. Human experts are not interchangeable; a brilliant strategist thinks differently from a creative innovator, even when solving the same problem. Their personalities shape their approaches.

We argue that truly powerful AI systems require not just capability but personality — consistent behavioral patterns that emerge from core values, fears, strengths, and philosophical orientations. BLACKWXDOW and JUMPER represent this synthesis: architecturally sophisticated MoE systems whose expert routing, integration strategies, and output generation are all influenced by their personality cores.

BLACKWXDOW's personality core is STRATEGIC_PATIENCE. Every aspect of its MoE architecture reflects this:

Calculation0.98Deeper analysis passes before integration

3.2 MoE Configuration

BLACKWXDOW employs 8 specialized experts with learned soft routing:

R(x) = softmax(W_g · x + ε), where ε ~ N(0, 0.1)

The routing strategy is conservative — top-2 selection with high confidence thresholds. This reflects BLACKWXDOW's philosophy: activate only the experts necessary, never more.
