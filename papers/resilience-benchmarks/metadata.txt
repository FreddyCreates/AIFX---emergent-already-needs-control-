Abstract:
This technical note defines four reproducible resilience measurements for governed agentic systems: (1) poisoning resistance (false-claim reach to memory), (2) boundary leakage (prompt-injection attempts to escape governance evaluation), (3) rollback integrity (corrupt-and-recover lineage graphs while preserving ancestry), and (4) promotion throughput (hypotheses/min under varying governance friction). The note also records a strict CPL-L sandbox policy that rejects unsafe `when` expressions and emits telemetry for rejected expressions.
